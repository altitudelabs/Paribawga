project scope
Restaurant and bar furniture
description
Inspired by the antebellum romance of pre-war Indochina, Rau Ram commissioned Paribawga to create a range of dining tables, daybeds and bar stools in the brand's signature burnt teak finishing and a series of suspended trays in a copper glazy for the bar area.
